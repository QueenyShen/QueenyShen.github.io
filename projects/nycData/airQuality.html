About the website

The data used on this website has been collected from the NYC Open Data, and specifically focuses on the annual concentration of PM2.5 during the years 2009-2010. PM2.5 is considered one of the most harmful urban pollutants, and is known to have negative impacts on both human health and the environment. The website offers interactive and interesing visualizations that enable visitors to explore and analyze the data in a meaningful way. I hope that this website will raise awareness about the importance of air quality in New York City and help promote sustainable and environmentally-friendly policies and practices.
